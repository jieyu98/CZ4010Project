Crypographically Secure RNG

Mouse Movement

Move your mouse randomly in the 256 x 256 pixel box on the left to generate random numbers.
Every time there is a change in horizontal or vertical direction, the script will take the x and y coordinates of the mouse pointer, convert them into binary, and concatenate them. This process repeats until a total of 2048 bits have been generated.
